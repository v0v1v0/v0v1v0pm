p.ancova_to_d1 R Documentation

One-tailed p-value from ANCOVA to Standardized Mean Difference (d)

Description

Converts a one-tailed p-value from ANCOVA reported in the primary study to a standardized mean difference (d)

Usage

p.ancova_to_d1(p, n.1, n.2, R, q)

Arguments

p

One-tailed p-value reported in primary study.

n.1

Treatment group sample size.

n.2

Comparison group sample size.

R

Covariate outcome correlation or multiple correlation.

q

number of covariates.

Value

d

Standardized mean difference (d).
var_d

Variance of d.
