r_to_d R Documentation

Correlation (r) to Standardized Mean Difference (d)

Description

Converts a correlation (r) to standardized mean difference (d).

Usage

r_to_d(r, N)

Arguments

r

Correlation coefficient.

N

Total sample size.

Value

d

Standardized mean difference (d).

Author(s)

AC Del Re & William T. Hoyt

Maintainer: AC Del Re acdelre@gmail.com

References
