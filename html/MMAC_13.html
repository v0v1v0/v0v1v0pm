FordMarketVolume1 R Documentation

Ford Motors Market Volume

Description

A subset of the dataset containing the volume of Ford Motor Company stock shares traded per quarter since January 1, 2007.

Usage

data(FordMarketVolume1)

Format

A data frame with 7 observations on the following variables.

  • Date quarter since January 1, 2007

  • Volume number of shares of Ford Motor Company stock shares

Details

This dataset contains the volume of Ford Motor Company stock shares traded per quarter since January 1, 2007.

Source

Ford Motor Company (F) stock market value quarterly in U.S. dollars. Accessed on July 7, 2014

References

http://stooq.com/q/d/?s=f.us

Examples

data(FordMarketVolume1)
