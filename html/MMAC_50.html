ToyotaMonthly R Documentation

Toyota Stock Prices

Description

A dataset containing the average monthly stock price of Toyota from 1982 to 1998.

Usage

data(ToyotaMonthly)

Format

A data frame with 60 observations on the following variables.

  • Month from January 1982

  • Value in US dollars

Details

This dataset contains the average monthly stock price of Toyota from 1982 to 1998.

Source

Toyota Motors Corporation (TM) stock market value in U.S. dollars from “Yahoo Finance | TM Historical Prices.” Accessed on August 13, 2016.

References

http://finance.yahoo.com/quote/TM/history?p=TM

Examples

data(ToyotaMonthly)
