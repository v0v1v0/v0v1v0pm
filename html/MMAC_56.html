USRetailTax R Documentation

US Retail Tax

Description

A dataset containing U.S. retail tax in millions of dollars each year from 2005 through 2011.

Usage

data(USRetailTax)

Format

A data frame with 7 observations on the following variables.

  • Year

  • Tax US retail tax in millions of dollars

Details

This dataset contains U.S. retail tax in millions of dollars each year from 2005 through 2011.

Source

Annual total retail sales taxes collected in the United States in each year from “Monthly & Annual Retail Trade, Main Page — U.S. Census Bureau.” Accessed on July 14, 2014.

References

http://www.census.gov/retail/

Examples

data(USRetailTax)
