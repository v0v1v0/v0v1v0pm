dept_data R Documentation

Dependent t Example Data

Description

Dataset for use in d.dep.t.diff, d.dep.t.diff.t, d.dep.t.avg, and d.dep.t.rm exploring the before and after effects of scifi movies on supernatural beliefs.

Usage

data(dept_data)

Format

A data frame of before and after scores for rating supernatural beliefs.

before: scores rated before watching a scifi movie after: scores rated after watching a scifi movie

References

Nolan and Heizen Statistics for the Behavioral Sciences (Book Link)
