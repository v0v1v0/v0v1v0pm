indt_data R Documentation

Independent t Example Data

Description

Dataset for use in d.ind.t, d.ind.t.t, delta.ind.t exploring the effects of hypnotism on the effects of recall after witnessing a crime.

Usage

data(indt_data)

Format

A data frame including two groups, one recieving a hypnotism intervention, and one control group, to determine how hypnotism effects recall after witnessing a crime.
