Data_City R Documentation

Data: City data

Description

City data for Operating Efficiency.

Usage

data("Data_City")

Format

A data frame containing data for 15 city observations

DMU

a numeric vector

City

a character vector

Houseprice

a numeric vector

Rental

a numeric vector

Violent

a numeric vector

Income

a numeric vector

B.Degree

a numeric vector

Doctor

a numeric vector

Source

W.D. Cook, L. Liang, Y. Zha and J.Zhu (2009) A Modified Super-Efficiency DEA Model for Infeasibility, The Journal of the Operational Research Society Vol. 60, No. 2 (Feb., 2009), pp. 276-281.

References

W.D. Cook, L. Liang, Y. Zha and J.Zhu (2009) A Modified Super-Efficiency DEA Model for Infeasibility, The Journal of the Operational Research Society Vol. 60, No. 2 (Feb., 2009), pp. 276-281.

Examples

data(Data_City)
attach(Data_City)
