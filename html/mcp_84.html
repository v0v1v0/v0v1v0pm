Description

Get the intercept of a formula

Usage

unpack_int(form, i, ttype)

Arguments

form

A formula
i

Segment number (integer)
ttype

The term type. One of "ct" (central tendency), "sigma" (variance), or "ar" (autoregressive)

Value

A one-row tibble describing the intercept.
