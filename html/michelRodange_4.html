sigfrid R Documentation

Full text of the Sigfrid play in Luxembourguish

Description

A dataset containing the full text of Michel Rodange's play, Dem Grow Sigfrid seng Goldkuommer in Luxembourguish.

Usage

sigfrid

Format

A data frame with 6471 rows and 3 variables:

line

Original text

act

Section of the play

nb_line

Line number

Source

https://data.public.lu/fr/datasets/the-works-in-luxembourguish-of-michel-rodange/
