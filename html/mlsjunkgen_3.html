mlsjunkgen: Use the MLS Junk Generator Algorithm to Generate a Stream of Pseudo-Random Numbers

mlsjunkgen functions

  • junkgen: generate a single pseudo-random number; called by the other functions

  • mlsjunkgenv: generate a vector stream of pseudo-random numbers

  • mlsjunkgend: generate a data frame of pseudo-random numbers

  • mlsjunkgenm: generate a matrix of pseudo-random numbers
