h_kr_df R Documentation

Obtain the Adjusted Kenward-Roger Degree of Freedom

Description

Obtains the adjusted Kenward-Roger degree of freedom and F statistic scale parameter. Used in h_df_md_kr() or h_df_1d_kr.

Usage

h_kr_df(v0, l, w, p)

Arguments

v0

(matrix)
unadjusted covariance matrix.
l

(matrix)
linear combination matrix.
w

(matrix)
hessian matrix.
p

(matrix)
P matrix from h_get_kr_comp().

Value

Named list with elements:

  • m: numeric degree of freedom.

  • lambda: numeric F statistic scale parameter.
