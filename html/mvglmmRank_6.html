2011 FBS College Football Regular Season Data

2011 FBS College Football Regular Season Data

Usage

A data frame with 781 observations on the following 9 variables.

a factor

Game.Date

a POSIXlt

away

a factor

home.response

a numeric vector

home.score

a numeric vector

away.response

a numeric vector

away.score

a numeric vector

neutral.site

a numeric vector

partition

a numeric vector
